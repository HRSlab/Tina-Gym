Questions, Answered

What should I wear?

Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing like yoga pants or athletic wear. Thai massage is performed fully clothed on a floor mat.

Does it hurt?

The pressure is firm but should never be painful. I will communicate with you throughout the session to ensure your comfort.

How often should I get a massage?

This depends on your goals. For chronic issues, weekly sessions may be beneficial initially. For general wellness, once or twice a month is common.
