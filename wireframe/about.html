About Tina Davis

A personal approach to wellness, rooted in experience and dedication to our community.

My Philosophy

My journey into movement and wellness wasn't just professional—it was personal. After navigating my own struggles with depression and feeling "stuck," I discovered the profound power of intentional movement to heal not just the body, but the mind. This experience is the foundation of my coaching.

I believe that everyone has immense strength within them. My role is not just to be a trainer, but to be a guide who helps you uncover that strength. We work together to build resilience, move through challenges, and cultivate a life of joy and vitality.

Your Fort Mohave Studio

I established this studio to create a dedicated space for the Fort Mohave community. It’s a place free from intimidation, where personalized attention is the standard. Whether you're a senior looking to improve your balance or an individual seeking relief from chronic pain, you have a home here.
